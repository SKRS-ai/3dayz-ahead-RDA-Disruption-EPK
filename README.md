## 🎧 THE SONIC MANIFESTO
RDA Disruption is a trans-Atlantic sonic experiment bridging classical European melody with American industrial grit. Their sound is defined by the "Synthetic Star" identity—a blend of digital precision and raw human passion. The upcoming debut album, ***Disruption***, explores the preservation of the inner child and the relentless pursuit of dreams at all costs.

---

## 👥 THE BAND (LINEUP)

* **Dante Vex (Vocals – Italy):** The "Gilded Growl." Melodic Italian roots meet Berlin industrial aggression.
* **Sloane Richter (Drums – Germany):** The "Metronome of the Revolution." Precision-engineered percussion.
* **Cypher Grey (Keys/Synths – USA):** The Architect. Blending complex piano with hacked digital textures.
* **Echo Grey (Guitar – USA):** The Shadow. Creating atmospheric "walls of sound" and soaring riffs.
